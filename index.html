Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create my own passport photo online for free?

Yes! Precision Print lets you crop and frame your photo to exact legal specifications and download a print-ready 4x6 sheet completely free, with no watermark or registration required.

What is the correct size for an Indian Visa photo?

The official photo size requirement for an Indian Visa or OCI application is 2x2 inches (51x51 mm). The background must be pure white, and the head should occupy 70% to 80% of the photo.

What are the requirements for a Schengen Visa photo?

A Schengen visa biometric photo must be 35mm in width and 45mm in height. The face should be centered, measuring between 32mm and 36mm from the chin to the top of the hair. The background must be light-colored or white.

How can I print passport size photos on a standard 6x4 paper?

Our built-in auto-tiling algorithm arranges multiple copies of your cropped photo onto a standard 6x4 photo paper. Once generated, download the JPEG and print it on a 6x4 paper size at 100% scale without borders.

What are US Visa photo requirements?

US visa photos must be 2x2 inches (51x51 mm) on a white or off-white background, taken within the last 6 months. Glasses are not allowed, and the head must be centered and sized between 1" and 1.375".
